Tref M&T is a corrugated cardboard manufacturer located in Kocani, Macedonia. Our experienced team has been working on designing and implementing the ideal solutions tailored to your packaging needs since our establishment in 1992.

Tref offers full service for your packaging needs: design, print, manufacture and delivery.

Our packaging simply blends with your product, making sure it arrives safe at it’s final destination, while looking extraordinary.
